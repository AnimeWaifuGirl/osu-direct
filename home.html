Why should i use osu.direct?

Fast, Reeeeeally fast

We know the pain of waiting about for beatmaps to download from bancho, or from other beatmap mirrors that limit how fast you can go. We don't do that here. You can enjoy blazing fast speeds, without the need of subscriptions or any payment.

Reliable and trusted

We used to be called Kitsu, but now we have a brand new, professional name about. As Kitsu, we have supported a large variety of projects. This includes Akatsuki, one of the largest osu! private servers, and o!rdr, the largest web replay rendering service powered by Danser. If they can trust us to reliably run, you can too.

Widely compatible, perfect for any use

We know how frustrating it is when you see something you could utilise in your next project, but is missing that one thing you need. That's why we're happy to add new features to our service whenever asked. We also support the v1 and v2 api routes, for ease of use and everything in between.
